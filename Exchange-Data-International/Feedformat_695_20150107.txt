WCA - Series 695 - Bloomberg version

This feed files can be regarded as a fixed format for all events if the generic definition is used.
The Dates 1 to 12 and Rates and Ratio, section vary from event to event in content, but not in datatype
The section Field 1 to 22 can vary in content and data type.
All variable fields have a descriptive tag.
All are varchar 255, but the real underlying datatype is given in this specification.

Primary Key fields
The first seven fields together marked with an asterisk are a primary, unique combination
Option and Serial IDs are not always applicable, but will then always be populated with ones.
